Welcome!

The Great Chicago Fire is the University of Chicago's invitational mock trial tournament. The second annual Great Chicago Fire Invitational will be held from January 16th to January 17th, 2016 at the Dirksen Federal Courthouse in Downtown Chicago. Unlike most other tournaments, the Great Chicago Fire Invitational features a bracket style elimination.

Learn more!

Logistics

Location

Our tournament is hosted in one of the most beautiful courthouses in the Midwest. Located in downtown Chicago, the Great Chicago Fire Invitational is held in the center of a historic and bustling city unlike any other.

Field

In our inaugural tournament, we hosted 11 schools, including our own. Nine went on to compete at ORCS, with four competing at the Nationals Touranment. The Ohio State University won our inaugural tournament, earning their eternal spot on our traveling trophy.

Bracket-Style Elimination

Our tournament is unique in that it features a bracket-style elimination. Each team is assigned to one of four brackets and moves through the bracket after defeating their opponents until one champion remains. Meanwhile, any team that falls out of the bracket still competes against other teams in the traditional tournament style.

Our Tournamenst

Some things never change. Despite the different format, each team is guaranteed four complete rounds of competition, two rounds each of both prosecution and defense sides of the case, and a chance at placing and winning a trophy even if your teams are eliminated from the bracket.
